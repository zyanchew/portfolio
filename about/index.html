✨ About Me ✨

Hi, I’m Zhi Yan Chew. A girl from Malaysia🇲🇾, on my way to becoming a global citizen🌍.

I’m passionate about sustainable urban mobility, resilient transportation networks, and data-driven planning. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from Tsinghua University and a Master’s degree in Architecture, Building, and Planning from Eindhoven University of Technology.

My academic journey has taken me across disciplines and countries, shaping a global perspective and equipping me with a diverse toolkit of computational and analytical methods. I have researched topics spanning urban mobility, travel behavior, and social networks.

🎯

My core skill sets include spatial data analytics, machine learning, and network science. I work extensively with large-scale datasets and advanced modeling techniques to uncover patterns in mobility behavior and inform equitable, evidence-based urban and transport policies. I am also experienced in Python, R, SQL, and geospatial tools such as QGIS.

Outside of work, I enjoy photography📸, arts🎨, and fashion👗. Never stop exploring!
