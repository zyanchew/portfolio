The challenge involved using a synthetic human mobility dataset to train a deep learning model for predicting future mobility trajectories. The tasks are divided into two scenarios, business-as-usual and under emergency.

üìà Results

  • Long-Short Term Memory (LSTM) Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) The LSTM-RNN is trained, and the model predicts the coordinates of an individual based on the day of the week and time period.

  • POI Diversity and Density The diversity and density of the POIs within a grid cell are calculated and visualised.

POI Diversity Heatmap: Represents the number of POI categories in each grid cell.

POI Density Heatmap: Represents the total number of POIs in each grid cell.
